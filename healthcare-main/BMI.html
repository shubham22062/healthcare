Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple calculation used to assess whether a person has a healthy body weight for a given height. It is widely used as a screening tool to categorize individuals into different weight categories, which can help identify potential health risks associated with underweight, overweight, and obesity.

#Heart Disease

#Type 2 Diabetes

#Joint Problems

#High Blood Pressure

Risk associate with underweight

#Nutritional Deficiencies

#Osteoporosis

#Weakened Immune System

#Fertility Issues
